## 3. Gig Description

**Professional Splunk SIEM Detection Engineering**

Installing a SIEM is only the first step; configuring it to actually catch attackers takes specialized expertise. I provide professional Detection Engineering services to turn your raw logs into high-fidelity security alerts.

**Technical Background:**
I am a Google Certified Cybersecurity Professional with extensive hands-on experience in enterprise logging architectures. I specialize in Splunk Enterprise, Sysmon, and translating MITRE ATT&CK techniques into actionable Search Processing Language (SPL) queries.

**Services Provided:**

* **Log Source Onboarding:** Assisting with the configuration of Splunk Universal Forwarders to ingest Windows Event Logs, Sysmon, and network traffic.
* **Custom Alert Creation:** Writing optimized, low-noise SPL queries to detect anomalous behavior (e.g., Brute Force, Pass-the-Hash, Suspicious Process Execution).
* **Dashboard Development:** Building "Single Pane of Glass" SOC visualizations to track security metrics, failed logins, and geographic attack origins.
* **Alert Tuning:** Reducing false positives in your existing rules so your team can focus on real threats.

Stop paying for a SIEM that just stores data. Let's configure it to actively defend your network.

**Please message me before ordering to discuss your log sources and detection goals!**
