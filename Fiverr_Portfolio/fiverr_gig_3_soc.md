## 3. Gig Description (1200 Character Max)

**Professional SOC Documentation & Incident Response Playbooks**

A successful Security Operations Center (SOC) doesn't just rely on tools; it relies on standardized, repeatable processes. When a breach happens, your team needs a clear, step-by-step response plan, not panic.

**Technical Background:**
I am a Google Certified Cybersecurity Professional with deep experience in Blue Team operations, alert triage, and security documentation. I write actionable, technically accurate procedures.

**Services Provided:**

* **Incident Response Playbooks:** Step-by-step guides for Tier-1/Tier-2 analysts to contain and eradicate threats (e.g., Malware Outbreaks, Phishing, Insider Threats, Brute Force).
* **Escalation Matrices:** Clear documentation defining exactly when, how, and to whom a security event should be escalated.
* **Shift Handoff Templates:** Standardized forms to ensure critical intelligence isn't lost between SOC shifts.
* **Customization:** All documentation is tailored to your specific tools (e.g., Splunk, CrowdStrike) and network topology.

Stop relying on tribal knowledge. Let's document your security procedures so your team can respond faster and more effectively.
