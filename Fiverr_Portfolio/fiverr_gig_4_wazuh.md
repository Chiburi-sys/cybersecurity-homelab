**Professional Wazuh SIEM & XDR Deployment**

Enterprise-grade security monitoring shouldn’t require a massive licensing budget. Wazuh is an extremely powerful open-source SIEM and XDR platform that provides threat detection, integrity monitoring, and incident response—all for $0 in software licensing fees.

I am a Google Certified Cybersecurity Professional who specializes in deploying and configuring Wazuh to provide you with a "Single Pane of Glass" view into your network's security posture.
